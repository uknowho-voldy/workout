Day A — Lower body
Tuesday · InShape Fitness · ~45 min
Hit 3×8 on squats → add 5 lbs next Tuesday. Log every set weight in Tracker.
Exercises
Primary lift
Barbell back squat
3 × 5–8 reps
Setup
Bar on upper traps, feet shoulder-width, toes slightly out
Descent
Sit back and down, chest up, knees track over toes, aim for parallel
Drive
Push floor away, drive hips forward, squeeze glutes at top
Compound
Romanian deadlift
3 × 8–10 reps · controlled descent
Hinge
Push hips back, bar close to legs, feel the hamstring stretch
Return
Drive hips forward, squeeze glutes at top
IT band note
Any knee discomfort → reduce weight, focus on hip hinge
Compound
Leg press
3 × 10–12 reps · quad emphasis
Feet
Shoulder-width mid-platform · lower = more quad, higher = more glute
Range
Lower to ~90 degrees · don't let lower back lift off pad
Accessory
Walking lunges
2 × 12 each leg · bodyweight or light dumbbells to start
Core
Plank
3 × 30–45 seconds · directly supports Adonis belt goal
Day B — Upper body
Thursday · InShape Fitness · ~45 min
Hit 3×8 on bench → add 5 lbs next Thursday. Log every set weight in Tracker.
Exercises
Primary lift
Barbell bench press
3 × 5–8 reps
Setup
Shoulder blades pinched back and down, slight arch, feet flat
Descent
Bar to lower chest, elbows at ~45–75 degrees, not flared wide
Compound
Barbell bent-over row
3 × 6–8 reps · back thickness
Position
Hinge to ~45 degrees, core braced
Pull
Drive elbows back, squeeze shoulder blades, bar to lower ribcage
Control
2 seconds down — no momentum
Compound
Overhead press
3 × 8–10 reps · shoulder definition
Option
Barbell or dumbbells — dumbbells better if any shoulder impingement
Core
Brace abs — don't hyperextend lower back
Compound
Lat pulldown
3 × 10–12 reps · back width
Focus
Think elbows to back pockets — not pulling with hands
Range
Full stretch at top — let shoulder blades rise. Pull to upper chest.
Accessory
DB curl + tricep pushdown (superset)
2 × 12 each · arms last — do not skip compounds to get here
